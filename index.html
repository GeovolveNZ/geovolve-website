Why Geovolve?

Geovolve is an independent New Zealand-based company that is focused on improving the geospatial capability and maturity of organisations through improvements in:

  • Business Processes
  • Data Management
  • Project Delivery
  • Geospatial Functionality

Geovolve's expertise is focused on organisations that manage assets in the Utilities, Environmental, and Local Government sectors.
We pride ourselves on the ability to deliver technology components as simply as possible while identifying business and data improvements that provide the greatest business benefit and broaden the use of geospatial functions.

Services

  • Project Management

  • Geospatial Advisory

About

Geovolve is represented by Justin Fail. Justin is an IT/Data/GIS professional with over 25 years international work experience across multiple business segments.

He is focused on improving people and processes via consulting, designing and managing the implementation of IT projects with a geospatial element. Justin has wide experience across both vendor-specific (eg. ESRI) and open-source solutions which allow him to deliver pragmatic solutions that best align with customer strategic and information management strategies.

His skills are best applied to organisations that need to implement and understand their business information via spatial and asset information management technologies.
